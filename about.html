About Alohomora Labs

Unlocking new frontiers in motion, gait, and biotech research.

Our Mission

To advance the science of human movement and biotechnology through innovative research, open-source tools, and a spirit of curiosity. We believe in making complex research accessible, magical, and impactful for the world.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the mysteries of motion and life are unlocked for the benefit of all. By combining data, algorithms, and imagination, we strive to push the boundaries of what’s possible in gait analysis and beyond.

Why "Alohomora"?

Our name is inspired by the idea of unlocking doors to new knowledge and possibilities. We approach research with wonder, rigor, and a touch of magic—opening pathways to discovery in every project we undertake.
